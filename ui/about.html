Understanding the Silent Threat

Cardiovascular Disease (CVD) refers to a group of disorders of the heart and blood vessels‚Äîincluding coronary heart disease and hypertension. As the leading cause of mortality globally, the primary challenge is that CVD often develops silently, with major events like heart attacks occurring without prior warning symptoms.

HeartSense AI addresses this by shifting the paradigm from reactive treatment to proactive risk assessment. By training on a diverse dataset of 68,748 patient records, our model identifies subtle correlations between vitals (BP, Cholesterol) and lifestyle habits to project risk trajectories long before clinical intervention becomes urgent.
