Interpretability over Complexity

During the research phase, we evaluated high-performance "Ensemble" methods (like Random Forest) and Neural Networks. However, for a medical screening tool, Explainable AI (XAI) is paramount. We chose a Single Decision Tree for the following academic reasons:

Traceable Decision Paths

The model follows a hierarchy of binary decisions (e.g., BP > 140?), mimicking clinical flowcharts used by doctors.

The Accuracy-Safety Trade-off

While black-box models may offer slightly higher accuracy, a 1% gain is not worth losing the ability to explain "why" a patient is at risk.
